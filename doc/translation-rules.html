ColdFrame: Translation rules

Translation involves transforming the metamodel into concrete code. Most of the transformations are discussed under the relevant section of the Architecture page.

There are class diagrams of the metamodel and of the mappings for

  • classes,
  • relationships,
  • types,
  • operations,
  • events and also
  • the container support.

The entities on these diagrams are discussed here.

Note, the diagrams and the document were derived from a model (in Rational Rose) which is no longer being maintained, and therefore aren't to be relied on.
